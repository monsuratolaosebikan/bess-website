Weekly Study Sessions

Study session takes place on Wednesday evenings and brings together all of our BESS members to form study groups and get tutoring for their engineering courses. Please contact our Academic Excellence Chair, Kasia Gibson, bess.nsbe.aex@gmail.com for more information.

Weekly Volunteering

Torch
Torch stands for Technical Outreach, Community Help. TORCH combines engineering expertise with community outreach through programs such a technical workshops to teach adults and K-12 students about engineering. BESS has a weekly TORCH program on Tuesdays at 6pm in Roxbury, MA. BESS members can volunteer to serve on a committee to help with planning and/or attend the weekly sessions. For more information, contact the BESS TORCH Chair: Asha Chen-Phang, bess.nsbe.torch@gmail.com!

Pre-College Initiative (PCI)
The goal of the pre-college initiative program is to increase interest in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math to K-12 students in order to encourage them to attend college and pursue technical degrees. BESS hosts an annual Pre-College Initiative conference for local middle and high school students. BESS members can volunteer to serve on a committee to help with planning and/or attend programming. For more information, contact the BESS PCI Chair: Thurston Brevett, bess.nsbe.pci@gmail.com!
